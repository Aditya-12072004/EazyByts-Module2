Trading Educational Hub

1. What is Stock Trading?

Stock Trading ka matlab hai kisi company ke shares (hissedari) khareedna aur bechna. Jab aap koi share kharidte hain, to aap us company ke chote se hisse ke maalik ban jate hain.

Goal: Stock ko kam daam par kharidna (Buy Low) aur zyada daam par bechna (Sell High) taaki profit ho.

2. What is Portfolio?

Aapka **Portfolio** aapke saare investments (stocks, bonds, wagera) ka ek collection hota hai. Aapka portfolio jitna diverse (alag-alag tarah ke stocks) hoga, utna hi risk kam hoga.

Risk Management: Kabhi bhi saara paisa sirf ek hi stock mein na lagayein.

3. Simple Moving Average (SMA) Strategy

SMA ek technical analysis tool hai jo price fluctuations ko smooth karta hai. Iska use trend direction aur momentum janne ke liye hota hai.

  • **Short MA (e.g., 20 days):** Price mein jaldi badlaav dikhata hai.
  • **Long MA (e.g., 50 days):** Price mein slow aur stable trend dikhata hai.
  • Crossover Signal: Jab Short MA, Long MA ko **upar ki taraf** cross karta hai (golden cross), to **Buy** ka signal milta hai.
